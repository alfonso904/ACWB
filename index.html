WEB DESIGN ‎ ‎FAQ

----

----

  • How much does a new website cost?
  • Our rate is $150/month with $0 down for a standard 5 page website for a small business. This includes web hosting, unlimited edits, 24/7 customer service, design for mobile, tablet & desktop, search engine optimization and web analytics. If more pages or specific work is requested, we can provide custom pricing based on the scope of work, time involved and number of additional pages. Contact us for custom pricing & quotes!
  • How long does it take to build a new website?
  • We offer a fast turnaround time for all of our clients depending on how quickly information and images for the website are received. A standard 5 page website can be completed in as soon as 14 days once information and images are received with the majority taking between 3-4 weeks to complete. If you need a website on a rush basis, we offer expedited services as well.
  • Do I have to be local to work with you?
  • Nope! Although we do focus our marketing and advertising within the Jacksonville, Florida area, we can work with you wherever you are located in the world.
  • What platforms do you use to build websites?
  • We meticulously hand-code all of our websites with HTML, CSS and JavaScript. This not only allows us to have an extremely fast website but also gives us limitless flexibility on the design and helps with search engine optimization. All of our work is manually coded and we do not use bloated page-builders or Wordpress like many other agencies use. Learn more.
  • What information do you need to build a website for my business?
  • First, we will ask you for a general overview of your business. We will need your business logo, colors, domain name you would like to use, number pages and if you would like any specific work done. We can work with the content from an existing website of yours as a template to provide a fresh new look for your website. Any descriptive information about your business and vision will help us create the most powerful website for your business.
  • Will my website be maintained and updated after it is completed?
  • Yes! Included in our monthly rate, we will continuously manage, maintain and update your website. Learn more.
  • Will my website be mobile-friendly?
  • All of our websites are coded mobile-first then we work on the tablet & desktop versions. This ensures your website looks beautiful on mobile devices which is where the majority of web traffic comes from today. Google also indexes websites based on mobile performance and gives us a significant edge on search engine results.
  • Will my website be seen on Google?
  • We submit your website pages to Google and use webmaster tools in order to get you listed on Google local maps. While it may take several months to a year to appear on the first page of Google, our search engine optimization techniques will make your website as "search engine friendly" as possible. Learn more.
