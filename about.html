About Us

Giving Small Businesses The Big Business Treatment

My name is Alfonso Espinoza, I am an Army veteran, a web developer and the owner of Atlantic Coast Web Designs. During my time in the Army, I was stationed in Alaska as an Airborne Infantryman. While in the military, all of my free time outside of the duty day was spent meticulously teaching myself how to manually code and develop webistes. Immediately, I gained a passion for coding and realized that it was what I wanted to do after my enlistment was over. Shortly afterwards, I ran into the idea of starting my own business specializing in small business web development.

While talking to various small businesses, I realized a lot of them did not have the funds to spend several thousands of dollars upfront on a website on top of other hidden fees. The ones that did, got taken advantage of and had a website with outdated & unresponsive designs filled with bloated code dragging it down. These businesses were in a frustrating situation - either they can't afford a good website and if they can, they don't know who to trust to not only deliver a great product but also provide quality customer service & support. That is why I believe in the all-in-one business model of $0 down and $150/month. It is simple, convenient and more manageable for a small business to handle without hurting their bank account.
