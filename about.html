About Us

Giving Small Businesses The Big Business Treatment

My name is Alfonso Espinoza, I am an Army veteran, a web developer and the owner of Atlantic Coast Web Designs. During my time in the Army, I was stationed in Alaska as an Airborne Infantryman. While I was in the military, I spent all of my free time outside of the duty day meticulously teaching myself how to manually code and develop webistes. Immediately, I gained a passion for coding and realized that it was what I wanted to do after my enlistment was over. Shortly afterwards, I got the idea of starting my own business specializing in small business web development.

While talking to various small businesses, I realized a lot of them don't have the funds to spend several thousands of dollars upfront on a website on top of other hidden fees. The ones that did, got taken advantage of and had a website that had outdated design, messy, cluttered, unresponsive and had bloated code. These businesses were in a frustrating situation - either they can't afford a good website and if they can, they don't know who to trust to not only deliver a great product but also provide quality customer service & support. That is why I believe in the business model of $0 down and $150/month. It is more manageable & simple for a small business along with not hurting their bank account.
